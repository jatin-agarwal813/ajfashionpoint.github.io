Accommodations

All accommodations have private baths and include Wi-Fi and breakfast.Unlike any other study abroad, we stay in highly rated hotels (not hostels).Hotels are located near the city-center and public transportation.

Home-Away-from-Home in Madrid

For the duration of the program, students will have their own room in a modern air -conditioned university residence, double occupancy, with private bath and free Wi-Fi in an upscale neighborhood. Each room is equipped with a refrigerator and microwave. In Madrid, students will be served both breakfast and their choice of lunch or dinner at the residence and have unlimited access to the on-premises fitness center and outdoor pool. All stays outside of Madrid are in highly-rated, city-center, hotels. Students will receive buffet breakfast daily when not in Madrid.
